A Men of War Game

by Emanuel Leston October 4th, 2015

Men of War franchise  is a series of world war two RTS (Real Time Strategy) games currently in Steam platform.  It is a game set in the second world war where the player deploy troops strategicly to defeat the opponent. There is a variety of troops within each unit(infantary units,armored units, naval units and air units) as well as game modes, e.g a set of different battles approach(Assault, Domination,.. TODO). In this game the player seats the position of the commander in the field ,launching his troops towards his goals to achieve victory.

The stage is set for the real time strategy game. Factions such as the USA, United Kingdom, Germany,Japan and Soviet Union  fill the game with all types of content and diversity putting the player the hard decision to pick in any context, e.g battle modes.  
All this factions diversity is upon their strength in the field. It is mostly define by aggressivenes, pressure and counter-attack. In some factions that lack in mobility you may find fire power or other perks according to it's units.

A Bad interface

When Windows 8 arrived on small portable devices such as hybrid or tablets at some point the virtual keyboard overplaced the type area.The user could not see what was typing.This problem is still today on Windows 10 a issue.It's not certain that in every app or web page it will be responsive.

Virtual keyboard interface it's particular mandatory on devices that do not support physical keyboards.In case of a responsive malfunction it can be frustrating hence decreasing user usability.
